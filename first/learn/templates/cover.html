Welcome to MethGET!

MethGET is a web-based software for analyzing the correlation between genome-wide DNA methylation and gene expression. Stand-alone version can also be available on https://github.com/Jason-Teng/MethGET.

Quick Guide

1. Upload data

  1. Enter Your Project Name and Email.
  2. Upload DNA Methylation (CGmap.gz) and Gene Expression Files (Exp.txt).
  3. Choose The Organism.
  4. Click “submit” button.

*you will receive 2 emails.

(1) start preprocessing mail (2) finish preprocessing mail (get account name)

2. Launch analysis

  1. sign in the account name (example account is provided).
  2. choose the parameters of analysis.
  3. submit (minutes).

3. GET results

  1. see results of CG, CHG, CHH.
  2. download selected figures and tables or just download all.

Note

  • Single methylome: check the checkbox of the sample and upload a CGmap.gz and a Exp.txt

    (0.5hr per methylome for Arabidopsis)

  • metagene: check the checkbox of metagene (3.5hr per metage for Arabidopsis)

  • multiple-methylome: need at least one methylome in group treatment and group control (trt_1, ctrl_1)

Input Format

DNA methylation data: CGmap

CGmap files are the output of BS-Seeker2 aligning pipeline that contain information of DNA methylation level, read counts and methylation context of each cytosine. The instruction for generating CGmap can be freely available from here.

Chr1 G 125 CHG CA 0.82 9 11
Chr1 G 126 CHH CC 0.18 2 11
Chr1 G 129 CHH CA 0.33 3 9
Chr1 C 135 CHH CA 0 0 12
Chr1 C 161 CG CG 0.76 16 21
... ... ... ... ... ... ... ...

Gene expression data: Tab-delimited text ﬁle

Tab-delimited text ﬁles contain the gene names and gene expression values. Official gene symbol is suggested to fit our build-in gene annotation.

AT1G01610 34.2495
AT1G01620 453.344
AT1G01630 53.2985
AT1G01640 40.7508
AT1G01650 36.3414
... ...

Gene annotation: GTF file

GTF files contain gene names and transcript annotation of the genome that can be available from ensemble FTP. The gene annotations for Arabidopsis, Rice, Human, and Mouse have built on the web.
